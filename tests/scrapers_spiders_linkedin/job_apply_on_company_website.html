      Role Summary:
      Engineering Trainee will be part of the team which is dedicated to the design of the turboprop engine components.
      The role is part-time with approximately 15 - 20 hours a week time capacity.

      Essential Responsibilities:
    • Perform design changes on components and subassemblies
    • Process manufacturing non-conformances from airworthiness perspective
    • Cooperate with production and suppliers
    • Document and communicate design solutions

      • Qualifications/Requirements:
    • Student of Technical University ideally Mechanical Engineering or Aviation specialization
    • Communicative knowledge of English, Czech / Slovak is an advantage
    • Familiar with CAD tools, preferably NX
    • PC proficiency (MS Office)
    • Good communication and presentation skills
    • Team worker with Engineering thinking
    • Proactive and eager to learn new things
